About Us


Our Story:


It was back in 2010, when I first heard about Whey Protein during one of my Gymming sessions. While the Gym was a buzz with discussions of Whey Proteins and the ultimate muscle gainers, A quick enquiry with my local store made me realise that not much was truly known about the product. Many visits, to many stores later, I realised that that even some of the best sporting goods and supplements stores did not truly offer much in the way of product information for their customers. So I simply set out to research what these seemingly magic protein shakes were all about because I definitely didn't want to be consuming something without knowing what I was having.


My passion for a healthy lifestyle had evolved to one where I wanted to share the knowledge and empower with people around me. An idea began to take root - a start up, a forum, a place where I would share my knowledge with consumers just like me. In April 2012, I started a franchisee of Fittgold in Pune. Fittgold a Mumbai based supplements store, dissolved business in Sept 2015. On sole proprietorship, FITTCITI (Synergy Supplement) was established on 1st October 2015.
